Create a City Site Using Drupal

Currently working on this site for my Drupal class.

LIVE SITE: Coming Soon!

Overview

My instructions are to "Build a city website based upon the content provided and the criteria as stated in the attached project instructions".

Question

How can I make this fun?

Goal

The goal of this project was to make a fun, yet informational site about a fictional city.

My Part

  1. Understand the users and their values.

    Research:

    I looked at several existing city sites in order to get a feel of what they usually look like.

  2. Define the problem.

    Make the given content come together to make an aesthetically pleasing and easily navigated website.

  3. Choosing the aesthetics.

    I was given word content to use. I was also given a few photos to use, but once I came up with the theme of the city, I used Gemini to create all of the unique images. I had way too much fun doing that!

    I decided to make this a city of frogs based on my love for them.

    My color palette is based on the colors of a vibrantly colored tree frog. Since I was using a few shades of green, I decided to make Frogville a very environmental friendly city.

  4. Design the product.

    The wireframes are being created in Adobe XD.

  5. Implement the solution onto a webpage.

    Coming Soon!

  6. Evaluate the work and improve it.

    I would use more time to look through more city websites to come up with my own content to add.
