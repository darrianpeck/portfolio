ABOUT ME

I have always loved to create; painting, drawing, and making too many DIY projects. I took a lot of time figuring out what I wanted as a career. Radiology was my first plan, then sonography. I realized quickly it would be a repetative job. I was afraid I would get bored, and I realized I am meant to explore my creativity as it grows. I took a few years to work while deciding what I wanted to do. I was a server and bartender; working on my people skills.

I never thought creativity was a trait to have in the tech world, so you can imagine my feeling when I discovered UX/UI Design. The term came to me on TikTok. I had no idea what it was, and being me, I had to know. After some research, I fell in love with the idea and figured out what kind of degree I needed to get a job like this.

So, here I am in the Web Development Program at Des Moines Area Community College, graduating in May of 2024. I know I am a design forward person and really enjoy frontend work. I have experience with backend work, and would love to learn more.
